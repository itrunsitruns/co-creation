What is Moonyou?

Moonyou 你的月亮是一款整合月經週期、間歇性斷食、與安全期／受孕期追蹤的個人化健康工具。不只是記錄經期——它根據你的週期階段，動態調整斷食建議、標示安全與危險期、顯示真實月相，讓你一眼掌握身體節奏。

Moonyou is a personalized health tool that integrates menstrual cycle tracking, intermittent fasting guidance, and fertility/safe period awareness — all in one beautiful interface. It dynamically adjusts fasting recommendations based on your cycle phase, marks fertile and safe windows, and displays real lunar phases so you can see your body's rhythm alongside nature's.

How to Add to Your Home Screen

iPhone（Safari）

  1. 用 Safari 開啟 moonyou.vercel.app
    Open the link in Safari
  2. 點底部的「分享」按鈕（⬆️ 方框加向上箭頭）
    Tap the Share button at the bottom (⬆️ square with arrow)
  3. 往下滑，點「加入主畫面」
    Scroll down, tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. 點「新增」—— 完成！
    Tap "Add" — done!
⚠️ iPhone 請務必用 Safari 開啟。Chrome 或 LINE 內建瀏覽器無法使用此功能。
You must use Safari on iPhone. Chrome or in-app browsers won't work.

Android（Chrome）

  1. 用 Chrome 開啟 moonyou.vercel.app
    Open the link in Chrome
  2. 點右上角三個點 ⋮
    Tap the three dots ⋮ in the top right
  3. 點「加到主畫面」或「安裝應用程式」
    Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
  4. 確認 —— 完成！
    Confirm — done!
