第一站：圍巾צעיפים מישראל

Dreadlocks 是以色列的自然系服飾品牌，以根源文化與大地色彩聞名。Signa Taiwan 把它完整地帶來台灣——從一條圍巾開始。本系列 56 款顏色，每一條都有自己的希伯來名字。

Dreadlocks is a natural-style humanswear label from Israel, rooted in earthy colours and a free-spirited soul. Signa Taiwan brings the full brand to Taiwan — beginning with a single scarf. This collection has 56 colours, each with its own Hebrew name.
